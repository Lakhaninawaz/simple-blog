About Us

The News Elon Daily is the student-run news organization at Elon University. We are dedicated to providing our readers with accurate, timely, and relevant news coverage of our campus and community. We are also committed to providing our students with the opportunity to learn and grow as journalists in a professional newsroom setting.

Our website and blog are updated daily with new stories, features, and multimedia content. We also publish a weekly print edition of the newspaper during the academic year. Our team of student journalists works hard to produce high-quality journalism that informs and engages our readers.
